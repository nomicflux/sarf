# Privacy Policy — Sarf

Last updated: 2026-02-20

## What data is collected

Sarf does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. It does not track browsing history, and it does not use analytics.

## What data is sent externally

When you hover over an Arabic word, that single word is sent to the [AlKhalil MorphoSys](http://alkhalil.oujda-nlp-team.net/AlKhalil-MorphoSys.php) morphological analysis service (`oujda-nlp-team.net`, operated by [Université Mohammed Premier](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S131915781630026X), Oujda, Morocco) to retrieve its grammatical breakdown. No other text from the page is sent. No cookies, identifiers, or metadata are included in these requests.

## What data is stored locally

- A dictionary database (Hans Wehr and Wiktionary entries) is stored in your browser's IndexedDB on first install. This data is bundled with the extension and never leaves your device.
- Your dictionary display preferences (which sources to show) are stored in `chrome.storage.local` on your device.
- Recent analysis results are cached in memory and discarded when the background script unloads.

## Third-party services

The only external service contacted is [AlKhalil MorphoSys](http://alkhalil.oujda-nlp-team.net/AlKhalil-MorphoSys.php) (`oujda-nlp-team.net`). Sarf has no affiliation with this service. Refer to Université Mohammed Premier's policies for how they handle incoming API requests.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at the project's GitHub repository.
